Chinkulu — Handcrafted Fashion Label

Chinkulu is a slow-fashion brand handcrafted with the support of local women stitching artists. Every piece is stitched by hand, making the brand emotionally meaningful and culturally rooted. My goal was to shape Chinkulu into a brand that celebrates real craftsmanship.

Overview

Chinkulu positions itself away from fast-fashion noise. It highlights slow, thoughtful creation — the kind that only community-led craftsmanship can produce.

Challenge

Local stitching artists have skill but no visibility. The brand needed an identity that translated handmade beauty into a digital-first story consumers would notice.

My Role

  • Logo & handcrafted identity
  • Narrative & brand messaging
  • Marketing funnel mapping
  • Story-driven content direction
  • Brand positioning

Outcome

Chinkulu became a brand with emotional weight — a bridge between craftsmanship and modern storytelling, built on authenticity and community empowerment.
