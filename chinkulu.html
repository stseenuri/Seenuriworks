Chinkulu — Handcrafted Fashion Brand

Chinkulu is a slow-fashion label built around local women stitching artists. Every piece is hand-stitched, imperfections included — making the brand feel human, warm, and culturally rooted.

Overview

Chinkulu was born from the idea that handmade work deserves a bigger stage. The challenge was to build a brand that respects craftsmanship while still appealing to modern consumers.

Challenge

Fast fashion dominated the market, overshadowing local artistry. Chinkulu needed an identity that communicated authenticity, storytelling, and community empowerment.

My Role

I shaped the full branding and marketing foundation:

  • Logo design & handcrafted visual identity
  • Brand narrative & storytelling
  • Marketing funnel & customer journey
  • Strategy for digital awareness

Outcome

Chinkulu now has a strong brand presence — simple, honest, handmade. The identity honors the artists behind the work and gives the brand a distinctive emotional position.
