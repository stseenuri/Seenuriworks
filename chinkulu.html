Chinkulu — Handcrafted Fashion Brand

Chinkulu is a slow-fashion label created in collaboration with local women stitching artists. Every piece is stitched entirely by hand using traditional techniques, giving the brand a deeply authentic touch.

I developed the brand identity, signature logo, and creative direction. The idea behind Chinkulu is not just fashion — it's empowerment. It gives local women a platform to turn their skills into a sustainable livelihood.
