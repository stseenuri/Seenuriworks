Zapwash — Hyperlocal Laundry Aggregator

Zapwash is a community-driven laundry brand built in collaboration with local dhobis. The goal was simple: give traditional workers modern visibility and give customers a seamless, digital-first service. I built Zapwash end-to-end — strategy, branding, identity, website, SEO, ads, funnel, and operational flow.

Overview

Zapwash operates on a hybrid model: “local trust + digital systems.” Dhobis get consistent leads while customers get structured pickups, clear pricing, and easy slot booking.

Challenge

Local laundry services rely on walk-ins and word of mouth. There was no consistent branding, no funnel, no lead engine. The challenge was to transform a traditional service into a scalable hyperlocal brand.

My Role

  • Brand strategy & positioning
  • Logo & identity design
  • SEO strategy (Local + Online)
  • Website design & development (thezapwash.com)
  • Paid advertising & funnel setup
  • GA4 integration & tracking
  • Service flow & customer journey mapping

Outcome

Zapwash started generating consistent monthly revenue (~₹2,000) through digital acquisition — without offline marketing. A complete blueprint now exists for scaling the service across more localities.
