Zapwash — Laundry Operations & Aggregator

Zapwash is a hyperlocal laundry aggregation service built in collaboration with local dhobis. I designed the operational model, onboarded partners, and built the entire pickup-to-delivery flow.

The brand currently brings in around ₹2,000 every month through orders that are generated entirely using online advertising funnels I designed.

I also designed and developed the complete frontend website at thezapwash.com, helping the brand establish a strong digital presence and convert customers directly through WhatsApp.
