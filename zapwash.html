Zapwash — Hyperlocal Laundry Aggregator

Zapwash is a hyperlocal laundry brand built by partnering with local dhobis and giving them a digital-first customer experience. I handled Zapwash end-to-end — strategy, branding, website design, SEO, paid ads, marketing funnels, and even the operational workflow.

Overview

The idea behind Zapwash was simple: combine the trust of local dhobis with modern systems. People wanted convenience; dhobis needed visibility. Zapwash became the bridge — digital on the outside, community-driven inside.

Challenge

Local laundry services rely heavily on walk-ins and word of mouth. There was no structure, no lead generation, and no consistent branding. The challenge was to build a full working brand from scratch: branding, acquisition strategy, funnel, pricing, and a functional website.

My Role

I built the entire brand system and growth engine:

  • Logo design & brand identity
  • Website design & development (thezapwash.com)
  • SEO strategy (online + local SEO)
  • Paid advertising funnels for lead generation
  • GA4 tracking & performance measurement
  • Service model & pickup/delivery flow
  • Communication & visual tone

Outcome

Zapwash began generating consistent leads — around ₹2,000 monthly — entirely through online advertising and SEO-backed funnel systems. The brand now has a scalable model that any local service can replicate digitally.
