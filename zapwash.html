Overview

Traditional service. Modern systems.

A community-driven laundry brand built with local dhobis. End-to-end ownership — strategy, brand, website, SEO, ads, operations. One platform connecting service providers with customers.

Challenge

No presence. No system.

Local laundry services depend on walk-ins and word of mouth. No consistent branding. No online visibility. No lead generation system. The goal was to turn a traditional service into a scalable hyperlocal brand.

Approach

Local trust meets digital reach.

Dhobis get steady leads. Customers get structured pickups, clear pricing, easy booking. Simple model. Clear value for both sides.
