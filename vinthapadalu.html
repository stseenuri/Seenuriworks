Vinthapadalu — Telugu Literature Community

Vinthapadalu is a modern Telugu literature identity designed to revive forgotten poems, authors, and storytelling traditions — but framed in a clean, modern visual language.

Overview

The goal was to make Telugu literature feel alive, contemporary, and emotionally engaging. Vinthapadalu needed an identity that honored the past while speaking today's design language.

Challenge

Regional literature lacks structured digital representation. Most audiences consume short-form content — so the challenge was to modernize storytelling without losing cultural depth.

My Role

  • Logo & identity design
  • Brand positioning & strategy
  • Content voice and narrative direction
  • Visual structure & page themes

Outcome

Vinthapadalu became a gateway for Telugu literature — organized, modern, visually consistent, and emotionally authentic. It bridges culture with contemporary design.
