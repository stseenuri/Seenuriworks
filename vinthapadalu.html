Vinthapadalu — Telugu Literature Community

Vinthapadalu is a modern Telugu literature community created to bring forgotten poems, writers, and cultural stories into today’s digital world.

Overview

The goal was to create a brand that felt nostalgic yet modern — something that honors Telugu literature while still fitting into today’s content formats and attention spans.

Challenge

Traditional literature often struggles to reach younger audiences. We needed a brand identity that carried cultural depth but communicated in a clean, accessible way.

My Role

I shaped the identity and content direction:

  • Logo design & identity system
  • Brand strategy & audience positioning
  • Content style & story frameworks
  • Visual narrative direction

Outcome

Vinthapadalu became a bridge between tradition and modern storytelling — a space where timeless literature feels accessible, organized, and engaging.
