Overview

Old words. New life.

Make Telugu literature feel alive, contemporary, emotionally engaging. An identity that honors the past while speaking today's visual language. Culture, modernized.

Challenge

Rich heritage. Poor visibility.

Regional literature lacks structured digital representation. Audiences consume short-form content. The challenge: modernize storytelling without losing cultural depth.

Approach

Design as translation.

Treat design as a bridge between eras. Take timeless content and present it through a lens that a digital-native audience understands and respects.
